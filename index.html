Vlogging

What is a Vlog?

A vlog is a video blog. Many people around the world like making vlogs.

Let's make a 30 second video or audio recording vlog and share it here with your classmates everyday!

A vlog can be about:

  • Activities you do 🤸‍♀️🧘🤹🎲🎸🧩🎮♟
  • Foods you eat or make 🍎🍌🍇🌽🍞🥗🍰
  • The weather ☀️🌧❄️🌬⛈🌨☁️🌪
  • Books you read 📕📗📘📙
  • Pets 🐶🐱🐹🐰🐔🐴🐦🦄
  • Things you learn about 📚📚📚📚📚
  • TV series or movies you watch 📺🎥📺🎥📺🎥

Once you've made a vlog you can share it here with your classmates!
